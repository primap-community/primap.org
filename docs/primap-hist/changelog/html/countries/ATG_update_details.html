Overview over changes

In the country reported priority scenario we have the following changes for aggregate Kyoto GHG and national total emissions excluding LULUCF (M.0.EL):

  • Emissions in 2022 have changed by 6.0%% (44.32 Gg CO2 / year)
  • Emissions in 1990-2022 have changed by 1.5%% (9.00 Gg CO2 / year)

In the third party priority scenario we have the following changes for aggregate Kyoto GHG and national total emissions excluding LULUCF (M.0.EL):

  • Emissions in 2022 have changed by 25.6%% (144.64 Gg CO2 / year)
  • Emissions in 1990-2022 have changed by 17.2%% (81.48 Gg CO2 / year)

Notes on data changes

Here we list notes explaining important emissions changes for the country. ’' means that the following text only applies to the TP time series, while  means that it only applies to the CR scenario. Otherwise the note applies to both scenarios.

  • 1.A, CO2: emissions are higher than CDIAC 2020 emissions
  • 2, KYOTOGHG (AR6GWP100): emissions in 2.D differ from EDGAR v7.0.
  • M.AG, KYOTOGHG (AR6GWP100): changes are due to the FAO data update. While FAO shows a decline in emissions for 2020 to 2021 it is not as steeps as in the years before and thus the modeled decline was steeper than the decline in FAO data.

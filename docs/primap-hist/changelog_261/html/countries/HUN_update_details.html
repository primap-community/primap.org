Notes on data changes

Here we list notes explaining important emissions changes for the country.

  • CRT data has been included but replaces official data reported to the EU which does not show larger differences. Changes in the CR time-series are small and come from updated third party data (EDGAR, FAO) which is used to extrapolate the country reported data.
  • In the TP time-series we have some changes form EDGAR and FAO data.
    • N2O in 2.B is much lower in EDGAR post-2001. CO2 is much lower for 2022 and 2023
    • CO2 in 1.B.1 is much higher for 2022 and 2023 in the new EDGAR data than in the old data.
    • N2O in M.AG.ELV is much lower for 2022 and 2023 which comes from a significant drop of emissions related to synthetic fertilizers for 2022 in the new FAOSTAT data
    • HFC emissions are lower post-2016 in the new EDGAR data.

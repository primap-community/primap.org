Notes on data changes

Here we list notes explaining important emissions changes for the country.

  • CRT data from BTR1 has been included covering 1990-2021. It replaces and extends data from BUR3 and DI.
    • The CRT data is much lower for the agricultural sectors (except for N2O in 3.A)
    • Energy CO2 emissions are higher in 2023 because the post-2020 growth rates in the CRT data are higher than in the EI data used before.
  • Changes in the TP scenario are due to the updated FAO and EDGAR data.

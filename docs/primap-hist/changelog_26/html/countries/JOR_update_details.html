Notes on data changes

Here we list notes explaining important emissions changes for the country. ’' means that the following text only applies to the TP time series, while  means that it only applies to the CR scenario. Otherwise the note applies to both scenarios.

  • We have removed agricultural CH4 data for 2000 as it is inconsistent with data for other years (from other submissions). This does not leave enough data points in country reported data for agricultural CH4, thus third party data is used in TP and CR scenarios.
  • Changes in sectors 4 and 5 are due to the removal of FAOSTAT data.
  • We have fixed a scaling bug in 1.B.2, CO2 which lead to very high pre-1990 emissions.
